How you can make this house possible:

That ideal home is a future prospect not easily attainable at the moment. However, that does not excuse any of us from doing our part right now. It is simple to move towards this future through any of these simple and easy steps.

  1. Recycle

    Even if it seems meaningless, it does make a difference. Not only conserving resources, recycling helps show the world that people do care about becoming wise in resource management.

  2. Invest in green technology

    Technology such as solar power is easily adapted. It makes up for its cost over time and actually saves you money in the long run. Not only that, but it boosts the renewable resource industry, allowing advances and even cheaper upgrades in the future!

  3. Independence is strength

    Don’t rely on things such as electrical companies. Always have a backup generator ready just in case something happens. Or better yet, invest in solar power and other renewable sources of energy. Possess a water filter in case of a flood.

  4. Educate someone else

    Spread the word! The only way we can change the way we can affect the world is if everybody works together. Education is a vital element of life and pretty much the way to improve it. Convince others to join you as you change your lifestyle. Maybe even start a local club or organization to help get the word out.

  5. Be more efficient

    Even the smallest changes are important. Turn off the lights and other electronics when you leave the house, especially your computer. Don’t leave your sprinklers running as often during winter. Try gardening with plants that don’t need any extra watering.
