A home of the future.

Now, more than ever, society is increasingly reliant on huge servers and central plants to function. However, is that really desirable? A single failure could affect huge numbers of people, crippling technology growth and economic well-being. The resulting mess might even leak into the environment, causing nigh-irreparable damage.

That is why the future that we envision is one based around a self-sustaining home. Mostly independent of corporations or power grids, this kind of house can survive by itself; it is powered by its own various energies and reuses as much as it can.

As a result, you have an Advancing Abode that has minimal impact on the environment and the quality of life. Sometimes, it isn't the complex that makes life better.
