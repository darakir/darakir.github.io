Information

The following are all components and features that help a home of the future function.


Self Generator
These can range from actual electrical generators to other, more ‘green’ counterparts like solar panels or for those with the space, windmills. Self generators can reduce your dependence on the power grid, promoting sustainability overall. Plus, it could be used to avoid blackouts! These devices will ultimately be easier and safer to access if servicing or repair is needed, while also reducing pollution to the environment.
Top

Grey Water
What is "grey water" exactly? Basically, it’s used water that isn’t dirty (like the water that’s used while waiting for a shower to heat up). It can be reused for a variety of things, like watering a garden. This will not only save water, but also money.
Top

Water Filters
Water filters basically take the actual dirty water, purify it, then recycle it back into the home for reuse. The system could potentially turn waste water or sewage into drinkable liquid! These are potentially the greatest contributor to water savings by reducing overall usage and cost, while minimizing impact to the environment.
Top

Solar Paneling
Solar panels are a great way to conserve energy through creating your own as an alternative to self generators; solar panels don’t require any fuel, just sunlight. The energy will be stored in special high-capacity batteries so it can be accessed at any time (and not only when it's produced). As these technologies continue to develop, an increasing amount of energy can be "harvested" from the sun.
Top

Greenhouses
Greenhouses provide a safe environment to grow various plants, and they naturally trap heat and even some water. In the long run, this can save both energy and water, while protecting plants against the weather. Whether it is food or flowers, greenhouses are very adaptable; the humidity and temperature can be adjusted at any time. The soil type can also be changed easily because the environment is relatively isolated.
Top

Greenery
Plant more plants! Plants naturally act to prevent erosion and help stabilize local climates. Planting a variety of species of trees or flowers is a form of land management that helps maintain the quality and biodiversity of the climate. Larger bushes and trees may even be placed as windbreakers. The beauty of plants is also another benefit to doing so. The more the better!
Top

Compost Piles
Compost piles are simply another way to recycle the materials we would otherwise throw out. By composting leftover plant material and similar foods, you can fertilize gardens, etc. that aid in plant growth. Another benefit would be reduced chemical fertilizer usage and reduction in the chance that soil and groundwater will be contaminated.
